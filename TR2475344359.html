Career Record/Resume/Certificate

  • What can Checking do?

    Checking has two main functions:
    - To verify TEFL certificates
    - To share personal profile

  • How can employers verify my TEFL certificate at Checking?

    To protect the authenticity of the TR TEFL certificates, each certificate is issued with a unique number which can be used to verify the certificate details. To learn about your certificate details, employers just need to enter your certificate number at Checking.

  • How can I share my E-resume with employers at Checking?

    Checking is like a personal database, more than an E-resume, that you can share with others. It includes all the personal information necessary for your job-search, including your ID, CV, certificates, teaching records, etc.. Employers can access some or all of your information with your permission, which is by means of an access code you provide in the system. It is such a convenient function that any sections or the whole of your online profile can be shared with only one keystroke.
